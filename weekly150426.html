06: Frequency Risk

The Fatigue Signal

The account has two distinct frequency stories this week. Obsidian is healthy. Adv+ Winners is a ticking clock. Last week's comms flagged Obsidian at 3.19x. Check where it's moved.

Obsidian set: still fresh

Individual Obsidian ads showing frequency of 1.52x (Vid 1) and 2.04x (Static 2). New creative (Ingredient Vid) at 2.23x is getting distribution. The audience hasn't seen these ads enough to tune out. The problem here is sustaining this freshness, not the frequency itself.

Adv+ Winners: 5.22x is critical

The full ad set is averaging 5.22x frequency. Even the Founder Talk creative at 4.96x ROAS is showing 3.15x on its own, up from the 3.19x flagged on Obsidian last week. The audience is starting to see this repeatedly. Performance will decline even on winning creatives at this cadence.

What 5.22x actually means

The average person in your target audience has seen an Adv+ Winners ad over 5 times in 7 days. CPMs will rise as Meta works harder to find unconvinced users. Click-through rates will compress. This is the exact pattern that preceded the Winners set decline in previous periods.

The creative pipeline is the fix

Frequency can't be solved by pausing ads. It's solved by introducing fresh creative. New ads reset the delivery algorithm to find fresh users. The Founder Unbox and Ingredient videos launched this week are the right instinct. They need to prove themselves by Wednesday.

Frequency action threshold:

Flag at 3.0x. Act at 4.0x. At 5.22x for Adv+ Winners, we're past both thresholds. The Founder Talk ad at 3.15x individual frequency needs a creative variant launched this week to share the load before it tips into decline.

08: Diagnosis

What Needs Attention

ROAS above target: first time in weeks

4.52x clears the 3.5x breakeven. The consolidation of Adv+ Winners behind stronger copy paid off. Obsidian continues performing above expectation at 5.32x ad set ROAS. This is the first clean week to start the scaling conversation from a position of strength.

Adv+ Winners frequency is unsustainable

5.22x ad set frequency with individual ads at 3.15x. The Founder Talk creative, currently the best performer at 4.96x, is showing early fatigue signals. If new creative doesn't start generating purchases within the week, this set will retrace. History in this account shows fast drops once frequency passes 4x.

New creative still unproven

Founder Unbox (5.68 spend, 0 purchases) and Obsidian Ingredient (4.04 spend, 0 purchases) are both in early delivery. These are the right formats based on past CBC performance data. But they need spend volume to show signal. $5-10 isn't enough to judge. Give them through Wednesday at minimum.

Scaling window is open but narrow

Three consecutive days above 3.5x ROAS is the scaling trigger per Todd's standing rule. This week cleared it at 4.52x. The window to incrementally increase budget is now. But frequency in Winners means we're scaling into a deteriorating audience if we don't move quickly on creative refresh.
