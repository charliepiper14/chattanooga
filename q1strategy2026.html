Budget Strategy

Q1 Budget Allocation Model

Campaign Budget Split

Advantage+ Evergreen 50% / $10,500

Proven performers. Todd videos, packing content, winning static ads.

Seasonal Campaigns 25% / $5,250

Valentine's push, New Year campaign, flash sales.

Creative Testing 15% / $3,150

New concepts, hooks, formats. Feed the winners pipeline.

Remarketing 10% / $2,100

Warm audiences, cart abandoners, past purchasers.

Spend Rules

Scale When:

  • CAC below $25 for 3+ consecutive days
  • ROAS above 2.0x with volume
  • CTR above 1.5% on new creative
  • Frequency below 2.5

Pause When:

  • CAC above $40 after $75+ spend
  • ROAS below 1.0x after learning phase
  • Frequency above 4.0
  • Zero purchases after $50+ spend

Daily Monitoring

Check at 10am and 4pm EST. Reallocate from underperformers to winners same day. Never let budget drain on losing ads.

Strategic Summary

Q4 proved the model works. $25,729 in spend generated $74,298 in tracked revenue with $48,569 profit. The path forward is not more spend but smarter allocation. Winners like the Halloween ad (10.77x ROAS) and Teaser campaigns (5.74x ROAS) demonstrate what is possible with the right creative and offer structure. Q1 focuses on replicating these wins while testing new angles like female gift buyers. Conservative budget increase of 18% targets 21% revenue growth through improved efficiency rather than raw scale.
