06 — Diagnosis

What Needs Fixing

Meta ROAS below breakeven

2.84x against a 3.5x target. The account runs profitably on a blended basis (10x MER), but if Todd looks at Ads Manager, this is what he sees. The gap is real — ads aren't self-funding on first purchase at current efficiency.

2 of 3 Winners ads fatiguing

HM Review static at 3.15 frequency with declining ROAS. Itch Intervention dropped from 2.79x to 1.27x in one week. Only Video 1 Copy is still holding in the Winners ad set. The evergreen backbone is eroding.

Over-reliance on Obsidian creatives

The Obsidian launch set is now carrying the account. Static 3 at 4.97x is the only ad above breakeven. If Obsidian creative fatigues, there's nothing strong behind it.

Low new customer acquisition

Only 21 new customers this week. The returning customer engine is carrying revenue, but the top of funnel is thin. The ads need to work harder at prospecting — and they can't do that on $37/day in spend.

08 — Creative Pipeline

Creatives to Brief

The account needs 2-3 new creatives to replace the fatiguing Winners ads. Based on what's working:

  • 1

    New static in the Obsidian Static 3 style

    Static 3 is outperforming everything. Brief more static creatives in this treatment — clean product imagery, direct copy, no over-production. Test a static for the core beard oil line using the same approach. If the format works for Obsidian, it can work across the catalog.

  • 2

    Fresh packing / behind-the-scenes video

    Historically CBC's strongest creative format. Label printing ASMR, order packing sequences — these have hit 5-9x+ ROAS in the past. Todd should film a new one featuring Obsidian product specifically. Fresh BTS content with new product = two hooks in one ad.

  • 3

    Founder-to-camera piece

    Todd talking about why he made Obsidian, what's in it, who it's for. Keep it raw, phone-filmed, under 30 seconds. Quiet confidence, not a pitch. This format aligns with the brand voice — founder presence through standards and conviction, not selling.

10 — Summary

Bottom Line

The business is profitable. $2,595 in revenue on $258 in ads. The returning customer engine Todd has built — handwritten notes, product quality, repeat purchase behavior — is generating real revenue that Meta can't see.

Meta ROAS is below target but the fix is creative, not structural. The campaign setup is sound. The algorithm is correctly starving bad ads and feeding good ones. We need to replace fatiguing creatives with fresh fuel.

Obsidian Static 3 at 4.97x is the proof point — the product-market fit is there, the audience is buying. More creative variations in this style, plus fresh BTS and founder content, will give ASC the ammunition to push above 3.5x.

Creative refresh first. Scale second. That's the play this week.
