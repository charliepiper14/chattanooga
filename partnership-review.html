The Journey: Key Milestones

September 2025

Account Handover

Inherited Advantage+ campaign with catalogue ads dominating spend. Split catalogue into separate campaign. Started new video creatives. Todd filming content for the first time.

October 2025

Halloween Flash Sale

Halloween ad hit 10.77x ROAS on $113 spend. Proved the flash sale model. Fresh ASC with concentrated spend outperformed everything in the account. Established the flash sale blueprint.

November 2025

Black Friday and Cyber Monday

Black Friday Teaser: 5.74x ROAS. BF TOF: 1.16x. Total BF/CM period: $14,992 spend, $23,628 Meta revenue. Cyber Monday BOGO free at 1.01x was the miss, crushing AOV to $41. Lesson applied to all future promotions.

December 2025

Christmas Push and Cleanup

Christmas Flash Sale: 3.54x ROAS. Christmas evergreen ads tested 8 new creatives. Christmas Video 3 and Static 3 both above 3x. Began scaling packing/BTS content that became the winning format.

January 2026

The Tough Month

$6,478 spend, $9,991 revenue. VIDEOAD1 ran too long at sub-1x ROAS. Interests a/b test and ABO test creatives burned budget. The account hit crisis point. Todd raised legitimate concerns about spend vs returns. Hard lessons on stop-loss discipline enforced going forward.

February 2026

Rebuild and Reset

Paused all underperformers. Dropped spend to prove efficiency. Valentine's Valet Drop: 2.36x ROAS on $581. Sold 19 of 20 limited bundles. MER held at 4.79x. New customer rate climbed 14% week on week. Trust rebuild started.

March 2026

Obsidian Launch and Flash Sale

Obsidian product launch: 4.75x ROAS. BOGO 50% flash sale: 4.14x ROAS. Copy test framework established with new Craig UGC and static angles. Account trending in the right direction with proven creative formats and disciplined spend.

What Won

  • Flash sales with fresh campaigns always outperformed evergreen
  • Teaser content with scarcity built pre-launch momentum
  • Todd on camera, authentic and raw, outperformed polished content every time
  • Packing/BTS/ASMR label printing formats drove 5-9x historically
  • Retargeting warm audiences on static creatives during sales events

What We Learned

  • BOGO 50% off protects AOV. BOGO free crushes it to $41 and kills margin
  • Meta ROAS understates by 40-60% vs Shopify MER, always cross-reference
  • Fresh campaigns for events, never bolt onto existing campaigns
  • Stop-loss rules must be enforced. January proved what happens without them
  • CAC:LTV (4.14x ratio) gives far more headroom than ROAS alone suggests

What to Avoid

  • Letting underperformers run past $50-75 without conversions
  • BOGO free on any future promotion
  • Fragmented ad sets splitting budget across too many audiences
  • Broad interest targeting without strong creative backing it
  • Editing live ads, always introduce new assets via new ad sets
